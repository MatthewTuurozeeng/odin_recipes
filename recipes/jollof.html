Ingredients

  • Uncooked Rice
  • Chicken Thigh
  • Cooking oil
  • Tomato Paste
  • Onions
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Carrot

Steps

    Step 1
  1. Place chicken in a pot
  2. Add onion, garlic, ginger, thyme, curry powder, pepper, seasoning cube, and salt.
  3. Mix well and steam over low heat for about 10-15 minutes (chicken will release its juices).
  4. Remove chicken and grill or fry until golden brown.
  5. Reserve the stock for cooking the rice.

    6. Step 2
  6. Heat oil in a large pot
  7. Add chopped onions and sautÃ© until translucent.
  8. Stir in tomato paste and fry for 3-5 minutes to remove acidity.
  9. Add the blended tomato-pepper mix and cook until the sauce thickens and oil begins to rise to the top (about 15â€“20 minutes).
  10. Season with curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, and salt.

    11. Step 3
  11. Add the washed rice to the stew and stir to coat all the grains.
  12. Pour in the chicken stock and a bit of water (enough to slightly cover the rice).
  13. Stir, cover with a tight lid or foil, and cook on low heat.
  14. Let simmer for about 20-30 minutes, checking and stirring occasionally to prevent burning
  15. Add a splash of water if rice is still hard; avoid too much water.
  16. When rice is cooked and moisture is absorbed, gently fluff with a fork.

    17. Step 4
  17. Serve the hot jollof rice with your grilled/fried chicken.
  18. Garnish with fried plantains, salad, or steamed vegetables (optional).
